Tepper & Bennett

Legendary Songwriting Duo

Musical Legacy

Roy C. Bennett and Sid Tepper wrote over 300 songs together from the 1940s through 1970s that were performed by many famous singers of the day.

Their partnership resulted in dozens of hits for Elvis Presley, as well as chart-topping songs performed by Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin, The Beatles, and many other iconic artists.
